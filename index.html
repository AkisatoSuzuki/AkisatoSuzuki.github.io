Welcome! I am half a data scientist and half a political scientist, being interested in applying Bayesian statistics, causal inference, and decision theory to find a solution to decision problems.

I was an Irish Research Council Government of Ireland Postdoctoral Fellow at the School of Politics and International Relations, University College Dublin, from 2018-20. I was a Max Weber Fellow at the European University Institute from 2016-18. I obtained my PhD from Dublin City University in 2015.

This website introduces some of my research. Obviously, the views expressed are my own unless otherwise stated, and do not represent the organizations/institutes to which I am belonging/have belonged.
